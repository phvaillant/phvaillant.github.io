Seattle Tract Travel Survey

This visualization explores the travel patterns throughout the city of Seattle through US Census tracts, transportation mode, transportation purpose, and hour of day. Hover on the map to explore popular travel paths. Filter by mode or purpose using the checkboxes. Data source: 2014 Puget Sound Regional Travel Study
